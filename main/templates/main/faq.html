FAQ

General Questions

All you need to know about our company and how to get support.

What Is REMIEL?

REMIEL is a member to member donation and mutual aid website for members to help other members in an efficient way. By using this website, members give and receive donations from each other.

What Is The Aim Of REMIEL?

Our aim is to create a platform that gives all participants an avenue to enjoy a higher standard of living and to avoid scams.

Why Is There No Referral System Or Affiliate Bonus Scheme?

I believe the difference between me and the others is that I do not believe anyone should make money off others. I therefore chose a system where everybody is equal and only earn from what they put in.

Who Can Join REMIEL?

Anybody of any age and sex can join REMIEL. Equal benefits and donations are assigned to all.

Other Questions

The answers on most common questions are described bellow.

Is Running Multiple Accounts Allowed?

Yes, you can run multiple accounts, but you must run them with different usernames, email addresses, and payment details as those are unique in the system

How Long Will It Take Get Downlines To Pay Me?

The time it takes for you to get downlines to pay you depends on the flow of new users in the system. To shorten this timeframe, this is why we introduced the autorecycle feature which forces new and existing members to keep investing in the system while retaining their profits of course. Downlines are assigned to pay usually on or before 5 days!

How Can I Make Payments Come Faster?

Easy, the more people who participate; the faster payments will come your way! So spread the word and get more people to join.

How Do I Receive Payment?

The downlines the system assigns to pay you will make payments via the payment details you provide while filling the registration form. They will proceed to make payment either via the bitcoin wallet you provided.

I Have Made Payment But My Payment Has Not Been Approved?

REMIEL is a timed system. Members are given 24 hours to confirm payments made by their downlines (provided valid evidence of payment has been uploaded). Once this time frame elapses and there is still no approval, the case will be automatically moved to the REMIEL Court where judges will preside over the issue and reach a verdict to confirm or cancel the payment based on the statements and evidences provided by you and the member you were matched to.

What if the Downline Assigned To Me Refuses To Pay?

There is no need to worry here. Once the time assigned to a downline has been exhausted, the system will block the account of the downline and assign a new downline to pay you within 24 hours.
